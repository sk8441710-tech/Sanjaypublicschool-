About Our School

Sanjay Public School, located in the serene surroundings of Nevrahi, Rani ki Sarai, Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh, is a premier educational institution committed to holistic development of students from Class 1 to 10.

Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), we follow a curriculum that balances academic excellence with co-curricular activities, ensuring all-round development of our students.

Our experienced faculty, modern teaching methodologies, and child-centric approach create an environment where students can explore, learn, and grow to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

We believe in nurturing not just academic proficiency but also moral values, critical thinking, and creativity in every child.

Our Academics

Primary Section (Class 1-5)

Foundational education with focus on literacy, numeracy, and holistic development through activity-based learning.

Middle Section (Class 6-8)

Building strong academic foundation with introduction to sciences, social studies, and languages as per CBSE curriculum.

Secondary Section (Class 9-10)

Comprehensive preparation for CBSE board examinations with specialized subject streams and career guidance.
